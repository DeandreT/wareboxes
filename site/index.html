Built for 3PL reality

One tenant.
Many owners.
No blurred boundaries.

A warehouse operator can manage multiple client inventories across multiple facilities while preserving the legal owner, authorization scope, and operational history of every unit.

Tenant
The operating organization and hard SaaS isolation boundary.
Inventory owner
The client or legal owner of stock managed by that tenant.
Facility
A warehouse where the tenant receives, stores, and ships goods.

Facility execution

From arrival to departure.

Wareboxes is organized around explicit commands and recoverable warehouse workflows, not arbitrary status edits.

  1. 01

    Arrive

    Appointments, loads, doors, and expected stock.

  2. 02

    Receive

    Scan, identify, inspect, label, and reconcile.

  3. 03

    Control

    Put away, move, hold, count, and trace.

  4. 04

    Execute

    Reserve, allocate, pick, pack, and verify.

  5. 05

    Ship

    Manifest, load, confirm, and publish events.
